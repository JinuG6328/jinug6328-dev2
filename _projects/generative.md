Inverse problems in science — recovering model parameters from observations — are
often ill-posed, high-dimensional, and computationally expensive. This project leverages
**diffusion models** and **flow matching** to build efficient posterior samplers and
design optimizers for scientific inverse problems.

We apply diffusion posterior sampling (DPS) to scientific inverse problems with
expensive simulators, and develop flow matching frameworks for real-time control
and design optimization {% cite wang2025plugandplay %}. Physics-informed constraints
are incorporated to ensure reliable posterior inference.

Applications include inverse problems at DOE facilities, power grid management,
and materials discovery.
