My research on BOED spans four interconnected directions:

**[Scalable BOED with Neural Operators](../no/)** — 
Replace expensive PDE solvers with neural operator surrogates (DINO, LANO), 
achieving 180–1000× speedup for large-scale experimental design.

**[Robust & Decision-Focused Design](../robust/)** — 
REIG guards against prior misspecification; GoBOED directly optimizes 
experiments for downstream decisions, not just parameter estimation.

**[Generative Priors for High-Dimensional BOED](../diffusion_boed/)** — 
Pretrained diffusion models as flexible priors; posterior sampling 
doubles as a tractable EIG estimator (MINDE-DPS).

**[LLM-Elicited Priors](../llm_prior/)** — 
Large language models as structured domain-knowledge priors for 
scientific inference and epidemic control.
